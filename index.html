Guess The Title of a WikiHow Article from its Illustrations

WikiHow.com is the internet's premier source for how-to guidance on virtually any topic, imaginable or otherwise. Since 2005, the site's community has been responsible for an ever-growing library of almost 200,000 articles that have been viewed a combined total of over one billion times. This is a collection of advice that ranges from the practical to the insane (and hits every point in between), all held together in a rich tapestry of thoroughly bizarre illustrations.

How to Play

The game starts off with two images. Submit a guess for the article title in the area at the bottom of the screen. You can have as many tries as you like.

Press to get another image from the article.


Press to reveal a word in the article title.


Press to start over with new images from a new article


Press to reveal the whole article title


Content

All article and image content is retrieved in real time from wikihow.com. Text is shared under a creative commons license. Images may be licensed differently. Specific licensing information can be determined by following the end-screen link to the original article, and clicking the image in its original context to reveal expanded information. The treatment of any copyrighted image material as part of this web toy is intended as entertaining commentary and protected under fair use.

Content

All article and image content is retrieved in real time from wikihow.com. Text is shared under a creative commons license. Images may be licensed differently. Specific licensing information can be determined by navigating to the image in its original context, i.e. the wikiHow article linked to the title that appears at the end of a game, and clicking the image to show its expanded information. The treatment of any copyrighted image material as part of this web game is intended only as entertaining commentary and is protected under fair use.
